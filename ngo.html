NGO in India to Change the Country's Landscape

Empowering the lives of backward and undeveloped people of the country is the mission of top NGO in India. Be it education, healthcare, job, environment, and many more concerns comes in the list of these NGOs. DevaMitra Foundation too is working for the same with their multiple launched programs of healthcare, education, environment and so on. With this, we have gathered the data of the main concerns of this sector from which there are two major concerns. These two should be sorted out first and at its best by our social welfare NGO. In this write-up, we will discuss these two areas of concerns and their solution as well.

We have two major concerns with their solution

Goshala

Once a cow grows old and stop giving milk, their owner disowns them because they are of no use to them. Thousands of such innocent cows are left on the street without a home. Many of these are just aged, injured, or abandoned. Some of these cows fall prey in the hands of butchers. Others are deeply starved and move from one place to another in search of food bearing the brunt of weather. These cows are insulted, canned and even pelted with stones. Some of these even meet with fatal accidents, others groan in pain due to severe injuries waiting for medical attention. The life of these cows become miserable.
Such cows are brought to our Gaushala from horrible time in which some are disabled or deeply injured even fighting for their life. All these cows are fed, sheltered, and looked after. Hundreds of gausewaks work round the clock to look after them. These gauvansh are fed with a mixture of fodder that includes wheat, mustard cake, green grass, bhusa (husk) and jaggery. They are given plenty of water and have acres of land to move around. We have a team of expert doctors who look after these cows and administer them quality medicines.

Senior Citizens

People live with dignity all their life and suddenly one day they are left with no choice but to beg to survive. In villages, many elderly are left alone after their children move to the cities in search of better livelihood. Many are there who beaten up by their own childs and thrown out from their house. Some aged people roaming on streets without even any slepper just to beg and suvive. Some are worked at this at many hazardous places.
So, for them Our NGO will work and make sure to give every help to aur senior citizens. Here, we will provide a hygenic food and a better shelter to them. Proper health care will given to them and there will be full body checkup twice in a month. Our one team will totally will look after them.
We are wholly committed for bringing social welfare and sustainable development in the society
