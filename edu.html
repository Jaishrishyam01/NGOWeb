HealthCare NGO

A healthcare NGO (Non-Governmental Organization) is a non-profit organization that operates independently of government control and is dedicated to improving access to healthcare services, promoting public health initiatives, and advocating for health equity.

Senior Citizen NGO

A senior citizen NGO (Non-Governmental Organization) is a non-profit organization dedicated to addressing the needs and concerns of older adults, typically those aged 60 and above.

Goshala NGO

A "goshala" NGO is an organization dedicated to the welfare and protection of cows, particularly in the context of Hindu culture and traditions. In Hinduism, cows are considered sacred and are revered as symbols of life, nourishment, and the divine. Goshalas typically provide shelter, food, and medical care for cows that are abandoned, injured, or in need of assistance.

Education NGO

An education NGO (Non-Governmental Organization) is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving educational access, quality, and outcomes, often focusing on marginalized communities or areas with limited resources. These organizations work independently of governments and collaborate with various stakeholders to implement programs and initiatives aimed at addressing educational challenges and promoting lifelong learning.

Environment Protection NGO

An Environment Protection NGO is a non-profit organization dedicated to conserving and safeguarding the environment. These NGOs work independently of governments and collaborate with various stakeholders to address environmental issues such as climate change, biodiversity loss, pollution, and habitat destruction.

DRR NGO

Disaster relief rehabilitation refers to the efforts aimed at providing immediate assistance and long-term support to individuals and communities affected by natural or man-made disasters. This includes delivering emergency aid such as food, shelter, and medical care during the immediate aftermath of a disaster, as well as implementing long-term recovery and reconstruction efforts to help affected communities rebuild their lives, infrastructure, and livelihoods.
